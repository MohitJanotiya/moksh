Frequently asked questions

Do your candles emit black soot?

Our soy candles are formulated for a clean burn. We use unbleached cotton wicks and 100% soy wax—no paraffin, no lead-core wicks.

How long does a candle last?

175g candles burn up to 32 hours. 80g candles burn up to 18 hours. Trim wicks to 5mm and burn until wax pools edge-to-edge for longevity.

How many fragrances do you have?

Six signature scents: Majestic Jasmine, Vanilla Breeze, Ocean Vibes, Floral Passion, Bohemian Summer, and Eastern Woods.

Are your products vegan?

Yes. Our formulations are vegan, paraben-free, and cruelty-free.
